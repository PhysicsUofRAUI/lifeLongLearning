What is this website?

We love learning!

This website has been designed to allow authors/tutors to display worksheets that they have made for potential learners to consume. The focus of the worksheets will be to quickly bring a person up to speed on a topic of interest.

A few worksheets have already been added to the website. At the moment there is no other features available except for authors to have their own account, and for learners to download worksheets. Eventually more features will be added. The building page has a list of features that are currently being implemented or planned.

For any suggestions on worksheets to cover feel free to email myself at kodyrogers21@gmail.com, or any of the other authors who have listed an email address.

If you want to become an author and post worksheets on this website email me at kodyrogers21@gmail.com.
