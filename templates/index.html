Who am I and what do I offer?

I love learning!

I was an Applied Physics major at the University of Regina, just finished my last semester. During my time as a student, I have picked up many ways to learn, and I would like to share them. Over the next few weeks and months, I intend to create some worksheets explaining physics and math concepts in a hands-on problem-solving way. The first worksheets will be available for free on the website, and the solutions will also be free, but I will only be releasing them via email to those who ask. Eventually, some more specialized and interesting worksheets, ones that answer exciting questions like those posed in the pictures, will be made available at an affordable cost. In the next section, I have created a timeline of what types and services will be released.

In addition to the worksheets, I plan to offer online 'coaching' sessions to help people to learn. These sessions could be in a regular tutoring format, or I could simply help with planning how to tackle learning a subject. How I envision helping is that I will guide you down a learning path with the work being done by yourself. These sessions will cost $25 / hour.

I am also open to any suggestions on how I can help you down your learning path.

I am available at the following email:kodyrogers21@gmail.com.
