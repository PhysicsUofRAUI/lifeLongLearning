What is this website?

We love learning!

This website has been designed to allow authors/tutors to display worksheets that they have made for potential learners to consume. The focus of the worksheets will be to quickly bring a person up to speed on a topic of interest. Worksheets will be made such that a broad audience will be able to understand the content. These general worksheets will be made available for free.

Eventually, worksheets covering particular applications will be made available for free or for purchase. An example would be an analysis of how important is the reaction time of a hockey goalie. Another could be an in-depth discussion about the planet Jupiter (how long it takes to get there, what would one-kilogram weight, etc.). These worksheets are meant to be both informative and fun.

For any suggestions on worksheets to cover feel free to email myself at kodyrogers21@gmail.com, or any of the other authors who have listed an email address.

If you want to become an author and post worksheets on this website email me at kodyrogers21@gmail.com.
